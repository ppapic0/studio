# **App Name**: LEARNING-LAB

## Core Features:

- Multi-Center Management & Onboarding: Allows administrators to create and manage multiple study centers. Onboarding of members (admin, teacher, student, parent) is handled via invite codes. Admin generates invite codes at /centers/{centerId}/inviteCodes/{code} (including role, expiresAt, maxUses), users redeem these codes during signup via a server-side Cloud Function which validates, activates membership at /centers/{centerId}/members/{uid}, increments usedCount, and logs audit entries. Granular, center-specific roles are defined exclusively at '/centers/{centerId}/members/{uid}' (no global roles at /users/{uid}.role). Supports subscription tiers (Basic, Pro, Enterprise) with explicit limits on max students, max teachers, AI usage (e.g., weekly/monthly quota), and data retention period, all stored at /centers/{centerId} along with billingStatus and expiresAt.
- Student Study Plan & Progress Tracking: Enables students to define their daily or weekly study plans, log completed sessions, and mark plan completion. All progress data is robustly stored and managed in Firestore at /centers/{centerId}/plans/{studentId}/weeks/{yyyy-WW}/items/{itemId} (with title, weight, done, doneAt) and study logs at /centers/{centerId}/studyLogs/{studentId}/days/{yyyy-mm-dd} (with totalMinutes, bySubject, updatedAt).
- Attendance Logging & Review: Provides teachers with an efficient interface to record student attendance, view attendance streaks, and review historical records. All critical data points are stored in Firestore at /centers/{centerId}/attendance/{yyyy-mm-dd}/records/{studentId} (with status, checkInAt, checkOutAt, updatedAt). Students can request check-in, but final attendance confirmation is by teacher/admin. Student write access to attendance is restricted to initial check-in requests, with teacher/admin write for confirmation and adjustments.
- Growth-Focused Ranking Leaderboards: Displays dynamic leaderboards based on growth-oriented metrics. Data is pre-aggregated and read-only from Firestore at /centers/{centerId}/leaderboards/{periodKey}/metrics/{metricKey}/entries/{studentId} (containing value, rank, displayNameSnapshot, updatedAt). Period keys follow YYYY-WW (weekly, ISO week) or YYYY-MM (monthly) formats. Metrics include: 'completionMaster' (weighted completed plan items / weighted total items; items without explicit weight default to 1), 'consistencyLeader' (primary: attendance streak; secondary tie-breaker: monthly attendance count), and 'growthChampion' ((last 7 days study time - prev 7 days study time) / max(prev 7 days study time, 1), clamped between -0.8 and +3.0 to prevent extreme values). Tie-breaking rules ensure deterministic ordering by recent update time, then attendance/completion rate, then UID. Aggregation for statsDaily (at /centers/{centerId}/statsDaily/{yyyy-mm-dd}/students/{studentId}) is triggered on write events, while leaderboards are updated daily via a scheduled Cloud Function at 00:10 KST for both weekly and monthly views.
- Personalized Performance Dashboard: Offers a tailored dashboard for students (showing today's plan, study time, streak, weekly completion, growth indicators), parents (providing weekly summary text, 3 key metrics, trend graphs for their linked students), and teachers (displaying risk student list, missing attendance, intervention queue) to monitor individual student progress, visualize key performance metrics, and review comparative rankings. Data is securely retrieved from Firestore, often from aggregated sources like /centers/{centerId}/statsDaily/{yyyy-mm-dd}/students/{studentId} and /centers/{centerId}/aiOutputs.
- AI-Powered Risk Detection System: A generative AI tool that automatically identifies 'at-risk' students by analyzing multiple performance indicators (e.g., plan completion rate < 50% for 2 weeks, 3+ days absence, study time growth rate -30%, 3+ days no plan). This AI operates on a read-only principle for raw logs (e.g., studyLogs, attendance, plans), never modifying original data. Detected risk flags and reasons are stored as derived data in Firestore under '/centers/{centerId}/aiOutputs/{studentId}/{docId}' with schema fields: type ('riskFlag'), message, a 'basedOn' snapshot of metrics used, 'model' used, 'createdAt', and 'safety' fields (e.g., for content moderation). It also generates audit logs at /centers/{centerId}/auditLogs/{logId} for traceability. All AI output documents are client write forbidden; only Cloud Functions or Admin SDK can write them. AI guardrails include: no medical/diagnosis/treatment advice; output is observation-based and avoids blame.
- Center KPI Dashboard: Provides center administrators with a comprehensive dashboard displaying key performance indicators (KPIs) such as monthly average attendance rate, average plan completion rate, average study time growth rate, seat utilization, risk student count, and re-enrollment projections. These metrics are aggregated from Firestore data at /centers/{centerId}/centerStats/{yyyy-mm}. Seat utilization relies on /centers/{centerId}/seats/{seatId} (label, status, assignedStudentId) and /centers/{centerId}/seatAssignments/{yyyy-mm}/{studentId} (seatId, planType, active). Re-enrollment projections are based on /centers/{centerId}/enrollments/{studentId} (startAt, endAt, status, renewalIntent).
- AI-Generated Parent Summaries: A generative AI tool that automatically creates and delivers interpretive weekly performance summaries for parents (e.g., 'plan completion up 12%,' 'stable attendance'). This AI operates on a read-only principle for raw logs, never modifying original data. Summaries are stored as derived data in Firestore under '/centers/{centerId}/aiOutputs/{studentId}/{docId}' with schema fields: type ('parentSummary'), message, a 'basedOn' snapshot of metrics used, 'model' used, 'createdAt', and 'safety' fields. It also generates audit logs at /centers/{centerId}/auditLogs/{logId}. All AI output documents are client write forbidden; only Cloud Functions or Admin SDK can write them. AI guardrails include: no medical/diagnosis/treatment advice; output avoids blame or definitive statements, focusing on observations and factual trends.
- AI Intervention Engine: A generative AI tool that provides data-driven, personalized intervention strategies and study recommendations to students based on their individual performance patterns and goals (e.g., if completion < 50%, suggest strategy + plan simplification; if growth negative, suggest time reallocation; if 7-day attendance, provide positive reinforcement). This AI operates on a read-only principle for raw logs, never modifying original data. Insights are stored as derived data in Firestore under '/centers/{centerId}/aiOutputs/{studentId}/{docId}' with schema fields: type ('intervention'), message, a 'basedOn' snapshot of metrics used, 'model' used, 'createdAt', and 'safety' fields. It also generates audit logs at /centers/{centerId}/auditLogs/{logId}. All AI output documents are client write forbidden; only Cloud Functions or Admin SDK can write them. AI guardrails include: no medical/diagnosis/treatment advice; recommendations are action-oriented suggestions; avoids sensitive topics like sleep/mental health.
- Notification System: Sends Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) notifications to students, parents, and teachers based on defined triggers such as: 2 days continuous absence (notifying student/parent); 2 days no plan submission (notifying student); detection of a high-risk student (notifying teacher/admin immediately, parents via weekly summary if applicable); and weekly summary generation (notifying parents). To prevent spam, notifications of the same type are rate-limited to once per 24 hours. Users can manage their notification preferences (enabled, channels, quietHours) at /users/{uid}/notificationPrefs/{centerId}.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Warm Brown (#5A4636) to evoke a calm, focused, and sophisticated 'study environment' feel.
- Background color: Warm White (#F8F6F2) for a clean, inviting backdrop reminiscent of a quiet library or cafe.
- Surface/Card color: Pure White (#FFFFFF) for cards, modals, and interactive elements, ensuring clarity and separation.
- Text Primary color: Dark Grey (#2F2F2F) for main text, ensuring high readability against warm backgrounds.
- Accent/CTA color: Camel Brown (#A07855) to highlight key actions and interactive components, providing a warm and subtle emphasis.
- Divider/Border color: Light Grey-Brown (#E3DFD8) for subtle visual separation without harshness.
- Success color: Muted Sage (#8E9A8A) for positive feedback and completion states, offering a calming and reassuring visual cue.
- Warning (soft) color: Border #C2A58A with background #FBF6F0, specifically avoiding harsh reds to maintain the calming aesthetic.
- Utilize a structured, card-based layout for dashboards and leaderboards, emphasizing clear data hierarchy and easy navigation within a calm, organized visual space. Information will be presented concisely, leveraging whitespace for improved readability and focus.
- Headlines will continue to use 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its modern, precise feel, while body text will use 'Inter' (sans-serif) for neutral, highly legible characteristics, ensuring clarity in data presentation, all aligned with a sophisticated study environment.
- Implement a suite of clean, minimalist line icons that align with themes of management, progress, and learning, specifically curated to fit the warm, refined aesthetic.
- Incorporate subtle micro-animations for user feedback, suchs as a smooth check motion for plan completion and a subtle upward animation for ranking increases. Warning notifications will use gentle brown-toned border changes (border #C2A58A + background #FBF6F0) instead of red, maintaining the calming aesthetic.
